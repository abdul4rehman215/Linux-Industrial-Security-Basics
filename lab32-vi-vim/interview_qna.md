Q1. What is vi/vim in Linux?
A: It is a powerful, modal text editor used for editing files in Linux systems.

Q2. Why is vi/vim commonly used by administrators?
A: It is fast, lightweight, and available on almost all Linux systems.

Q3. What are the two main modes used in this lab?
A: Normal mode and Insert mode.

Q4. How do you enter Insert mode in vi?
A: By pressing the i key.

Q5. How do you return to Normal mode?
A: By pressing the Esc key.

Q6. How do you save a file in vi?
A: By typing :w in Normal mode.

Q7. How do you quit vi?
A: By typing :q.

Q8. How do you save and quit at the same time?
A: By typing :wq.

Q9. How do you search for text in vi?
A: By typing /text and pressing Enter.

Q10. How do you replace all occurrences of a word in a file?
A: By using :%s/old/new/g.
